Your AI-UX Implementation Roadmap

1

Assess & Audit (Weeks 1-2)

  • • Evaluate current UX processes and tools
  • • Identify repetitive tasks suitable for AI automation
  • • Assess team's AI readiness and skill gaps
  • • Define success metrics and KPIs
2

Pilot Project (Weeks 3-6)

  • • Select one UX domain for initial AI integration
  • • Train team on chosen AI tools and workflows
  • • Run small-scale experiment with clear objectives
  • • Document learnings and iterate quickly
3

Scale & Expand (Weeks 7-12)

  • • Expand successful AI implementations to other domains
  • • Develop internal AI-UX best practices and guidelines
  • • Create cross-functional AI-human workflows
  • • Establish continuous learning and improvement cycles
4

Optimize & Evolve (Ongoing)

  • • Continuously monitor and optimize AI performance
  • • Stay updated with emerging AI-UX tools and techniques
  • • Foster a culture of experimentation and innovation
  • • Share learnings and best practices across the organization
